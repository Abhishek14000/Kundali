Type: Self-contained Raja Yoga (H5 + H10 lord in exaltation)

Venus's exaltation (Pisces = H3) places this Raja Yoga Karaka in a positive but deferred expression position. H3 is the 11th from H5 (gains from creativity) and the 6th from H10 (challenges in career). Active Adult Life (2024–2042, Rahu Mahadasha)

Middle Maturity (2042–2058, Jupiter Mahadasha)

The Viparita element (12th lord in 6th) suggests that conventional measures of success may transform — the native may move toward roles that are advisory, educational, or spiritually oriented rather than purely material.

Mature Harvest (2058–2077, Saturn Mahadasha)

Saturn's 19-year mahadasha as the vargottama lagna lord is the ultimate life harvest. This period (ages 56–75) consolidates all that has been built. Saturn in Rohini (abundance) in H5, with vargottama strength, and with D-9 placement in H9 (dharma house) = wisdom, authority, material security, and deep peace. This is when Himanshu will be the most himself: seasoned, wise, accomplished, and genuinely helpful to others.

  • Navigate baadhaka consciously: Know that Mars and Ketu are baadhaka lords — when their sub-periods arrive within Rahu MD, take a conservative, cautious approach to gains and property decisions.
